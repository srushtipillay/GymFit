Training Ability

The training abilities you cater to will play a part in who joins your gym. If you focus on high performance and improving personal bests in a specific area, an entry-level gym-goer isn’t going to sign up. Walking uphill, jogging, or running.

Incline walking is a low-impact way to torch calories without putting a strain on your joints. It's also great for people recovering from injury or who find running hard or boring. fit and is an excellent way to lose weight and build muscle mass.

High-impact aerobic dancing.

High-impact dance aerobics includes elements such as jumping jacks, simulated jump-roping and other forms of leaping with both feet off the ground. Step-aerobic moves can be either low or high-impact, depending on the speed and height of the steps you are using.
